Shipping Policy

At Regal Time, we ensure your luxury timepieces are delivered with the utmost care and security.

Domestic Shipping

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - $15 (Free on orders over $500)
  • Express Shipping: 2 business days - $25
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day - $50

International Shipping

  • Standard International: 7-14 business days - $50
  • Express International: 3-5 business days - $100

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Restrictions

Due to the high value of our products, we require signature confirmation for all deliveries. We ship to most countries but may be restricted in some regions due to customs regulations.

Tracking Your Order

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. For luxury watches, we provide additional insurance and special handling.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Regal Time purchase.

Return Period

You may return unworn, undamaged watches within 14 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service to initiate a return
  2. We'll provide a prepaid return shipping label
  3. Package the watch securely in its original packaging
  4. Include all original paperwork and accessories
  5. Ship the package using our provided label

Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges

For exchanges, please contact our customer service team. We'll guide you through the process and ensure you receive your desired timepiece promptly.

Non-Returnable Items

Personalized or engraved watches cannot be returned unless defective. All sales of vintage and limited edition pieces are final.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my watch is authentic?

All watches sold by Regal Time come with a certificate of authenticity and full manufacturer's warranty. We source directly from authorized dealers and brands to guarantee authenticity.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and Regal Time gift cards.

Do you offer international warranty?

Yes, most of our watches come with international manufacturer's warranty. Please check the specific product page for warranty details.

How often should I service my watch?

Mechanical watches should be serviced every 3-5 years, while quartz watches should be serviced every 5-7 years. Our customer service can help schedule maintenance.

Can I get my watch engraved?

Yes, we offer complimentary engraving on most models. Please contact us before placing your order to arrange this service.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we provide premium gift wrapping at no additional cost. You can select this option during checkout.
